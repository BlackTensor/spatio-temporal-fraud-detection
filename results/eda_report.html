Key Findings

  • Heavy class imbalance: only 9.76% of labelled nodes are illicit — requires weighted loss or oversampling.
  • Large unknown set: 157,205 nodes (77.1%) have no label — semi-supervised or transductive strategies needed.
  • No missing values: dataset is clean and ready for Phase 1.3 processing.
  • No duplicate edges: edge list is already deduplicated.
  • Power-law degree distribution: most nodes have degree 1–2; a few hubs have very high degree — hub nodes may be informative features.
  • Temporal consistency: 49 time steps with roughly stable node counts per step — suitable for snapshot-based temporal GNN.
